# Super Store Sales Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Problem Statement
Analyze sales data to identify trends, top products, and profit areas.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
  

## 📊 Dashboard Preview
![Super Store Sales Dashboard](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a8d1d1c9-4bd1-420b-9424-cc31ad71f396)


## 🔍 Key Insights
 Total Sales reached ₹2.08 Lakhs indicating strong business performance
• Oceania region contributes the highest share of sales
• Technology category generates around 50% of total profit
• Sales show fluctuations but an overall upward trend
• Top 5 products contribute significantly to total revenue

## ✅ Conclusion
Business should reduce discounts and focus on high-profit products.
